Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-20

Lone Star ITS collects only the information visitors voluntarily submit through the contact form, such as name, email address, selected service, and message details. Do not submit passwords, access codes, private keys, payment card numbers, health information, or other sensitive credentials through the website.

Form submissions are processed by Formspree and may be delivered to Lone Star ITS by email. Basic technical data, such as IP address, browser details, and request metadata, may be processed by GitHub Pages, Squarespace DNS, Cloudflare, Formspree, or other infrastructure providers needed to operate the site.

Information is used to respond to inquiries, provide requested services, prevent abuse, and maintain business records. Lone Star ITS does not sell website contact-form submissions.

To request access, correction, or deletion of information submitted through the site, contact Lone Star ITS through the Contact page.

This policy is a practical website privacy notice and should be reviewed by qualified counsel before use in regulated industries or jurisdictions with specific privacy obligations.
